Claim:  Taiwanese fresh milk is mostly pasteurized at low temperatures. （HTST/LTLT）\n
Reality: Most products on the market are actually UHT (which can be verified by the packaging label).\n
Impact: Consumers have misunderstandings about quality and shelf life.\n

How to verify:
1. Pick up any brand of fresh milk from the supermarket.
2. Check the sterilization method on the packaging (usually labeled UHT or Ultra-High Temperature).
3. Compare with the official claims.
