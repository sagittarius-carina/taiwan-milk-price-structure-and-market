Market narrative of milk industry  
Claim:  Taiwanese fresh milk is mostly pasteurized at low temperatures.(HTST/LTLT)  
Reality: Most products near 90% on the market are actually UHT(which can be verified by the packaging label) 
Endorsed by authoritative institutions: Taiwan Ministry of Agriculture, Consumers' Foundation
Impact: Consumers have misunderstandings about quality and shelf life.  

How to verify:
1. Pick up any brand of fresh milk from the supermarket.
2. Check the sterilization method on the packaging (usually labeled UHT or Ultra-High Temperature).
3. Search for articles related to the Ministry of Agriculture and the Consumers' Foundation using keywords.
4. Compare with the official claims.

乳品業界的市場敘事  
Claim: 台灣鮮乳多為低溫殺菌（HTST/LTLT）  
Reality: 市面上近90%的多數產品實際為UHT（可由包裝標示驗證）  
Endorsed by authoritative institutions: 台灣農業部, 消基會
Impact: 消費者對品質與保存期限產生誤解  

驗證方法:
1. 到超市拿任一品牌鮮乳
2. 查看包裝上的殺菌方式（通常標示UHT或超高溫）
3. 用關鍵字搜尋農業部, 消基會相關文章
4. 對照官方宣稱
