# Taiwan Milk Market - Q&A

## Why is milk expensive in Taiwan?

Milk prices remain high due to:
- Limited import volume (~10%)
- Strong domestic supply dominance
- Retail pricing structure

---

## Will zero tariffs reduce prices?

Not necessarily.

- Tariffs affect cost, but not total supply  
- Without sufficient volume, price impact is limited  

---

## Is imported milk lower quality?

No.

- It follows international safety standards  
- Shelf life differences come from processing and packaging  

---

## Can consumers reduce prices by switching products?

Partially.

- Individual behavior has limited impact  
- Structural supply constraints remain dominant
